# Code of Conduct

This project aims to stay respectful, practical, and focused.

## Expected behavior

Please:
- be respectful and direct
- criticize ideas, not people
- provide useful bug reports and review feedback
- keep disagreements technical and specific
- assume good faith unless there is strong evidence otherwise

## Unacceptable behavior

This includes:
- harassment or insults
- personal attacks
- repeated bad-faith disruption
- spam or deliberately low-effort noise
- hostile or abusive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions

## Maintainer enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, close, or remove content that damages the project environment, and may block repeat offenders if needed.
