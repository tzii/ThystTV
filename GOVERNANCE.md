# Governance

## Maintainer

ThystTV is currently maintained by `@tzii`.

## Decision model

This is a maintainer-led project.
The maintainer has final say on:
- roadmap
- release decisions
- architecture
- upstream sync strategy
- merge decisions

## Contribution model

Contributions are welcome through issues and pull requests.

High-value contributions are:
- clearly scoped
- testable
- well explained
- aligned with ThystTV priorities
- respectful of long-term maintainability

## Current priorities

Priority order:
1. stability and regressions
2. player UX
3. visual polish
4. large-screen quality
5. selective upstream improvements
6. new features

## Becoming a regular contributor

Regular contributors may be trusted more over time based on:
- quality of changes
- reviewability
- reliability
- communication
- care around regressions
